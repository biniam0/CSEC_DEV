Angela's Cinnamon Roll Recipe

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup butter

Instructions
